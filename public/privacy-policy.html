Your Rights & Security

What information do we collect?

To provide and improve our service, we collect a few types of information. We've broken them down for you below.

  • Text Descriptions: When you "Describe your post," we collect the text you enter to generate captions.
  • Image Uploads: When you "Add an image," we collect the photo data from your device's camera or gallery to help generate relevant content.
  • IP Address: Your device's unique address on the internet.
  • Device Information: Type of device, operating system, and browser used.
  • Usage Data: Basic information about how you interact with the app, like features used or time spent.
  • Camera: To allow you to take new photos for upload.
  • Read Media/Storage: To allow you to select existing photos from your device's gallery.
  • Internet/Network State: To connect to our web service and the AI models.

Who do we share your information with?

We limit data sharing to the essential third-party services that power our app. We do not sell your personal data.

With Google Gemini API

The text and image data you provide for caption generation are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. This data is processed transiently (we don't store it) and is subject to Google's privacy policy.

With Vercel

Our web application is hosted on Vercel. Standard server logs and analytics may be processed by Vercel as part of their hosting services.

We may disclose your information if required by law or in the event of a merger or acquisition. We will notify you if your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
